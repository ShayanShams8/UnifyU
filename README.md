## 🌍 Problem

International students struggle with:

- ❌ Understanding exams (SAT, TOEFL, etc.)
- ❌ Navigating visas and applications  
- ❌ Finding reliable, relatable advice  
- ❌ Accessing financial opportunities  

💡 Information is fragmented across Reddit, Quora, and forums — often unreliable.

---

## 💡 Solution

**UnifyU** combines:

- 📖 Real student experiences (blogs)
- 🤖 AI-powered assistant (**UnifyAI**)
- 💸 Financial discovery tools  

Users can:
- Learn from real student journeys  
- Ask questions and get contextual AI answers  
- Discover scholarships and opportunities  
- Connect with similar students  

---

## 🤖 How We Use AI

**UnifyAI is not just a chatbot.**

It:
- Pulls from **internal community blog data**
- Augments with **external sources (Reddit, Quora)**
- Suggests **real users to connect with**
- Focuses on **guidance, not decision-making**

---

## ❓ Hackathon Core Questions

### 1. Who is this for?
International students entering the U.S. education system.

### 2. What could go wrong?
- Misinformation  
- Privacy concerns  
- Over-reliance on AI  

### 3. How do we prevent this?
- Verified + community-driven data  
- Transparent AI limitations  
- Encouraging human-to-human connections  

---

## 🧩 Core Features

### 📚 Blog System
- Share experiences (visa, exams, campus life)
- Anonymous or public posting
- Threaded comments system

### 🤖 UnifyAI
- Context-aware AI assistant
- Suggests people + answers questions

### 💰 Financial Tools
- Scholarship scraping (Playwright)
- Job & opportunity discovery
- Currency conversion

### 🔐 Admin Panel
- Manage users/posts
- Moderate harmful content

---

## ⚖️ Ethical Considerations

We designed UnifyU to **empower, not replace users**:

- 🔒 Privacy-first design  
- 🧠 AI transparency  
- ⚠️ Bias awareness  
- 👥 Encourages real human connections  

---

## 📈 Impact

UnifyU aims to:

- 🌍 Increase access to opportunity  
- 🎓 Reduce confusion for international students  
- 🤝 Build a global support network
